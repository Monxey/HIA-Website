Our Mission

We are a nonprofit organization committed to making a lasting, positive impact in the College Park community. We address the needs of financially underserved individuals and families through year-round initiatives, including clothing and food drives, holiday gift programs, and educational supply distributions. By partnering with local homeless shelters and organizing community outreach events, we strive to ensure the well-being of all people—regardless of age, gender, or background.
